## Overview

This section contains all business planning documents for Bornara AI, operating as a Canadian sole
proprietorship. It covers the full business strategy across all revenue streams:

1. **Giftifye.com** — Shopify gift store (curated bundles, personalized gifts)
2. **AI Agent Platform** — SaaS tool for custom AI assistants
3. **Tech Consulting & AI Automation** — Small business services
4. **Cookie Business** — Baking, packaging, and shipping via Shopify
