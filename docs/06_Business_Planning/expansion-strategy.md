## 2. Stream 1: Giftifye.com (Shopify Dropshipping + Curated Gifts)

### Giftifye Startup Steps

1. **Niche Selection:** Aesthetic curated gift bundles (evergreen + seasonal)
2. **Supplier Research:**
   - AliExpress / CJ Dropshipping for dropship items
   - Local wholesalers for curated bundle components
   - Print-on-demand for personalized items (Printful, Printify)
3. **Store Setup:**
   - Shopify Basic plan ($39/month)
   - Professional theme (free or $150 one-time)
   - Apps: Oberlo/DSers (dropship), Klaviyo (email), Reviews, Upsell
4. **Product Selection:** Start with 20-30 items across categories:
   - Birthday bundles
   - Anniversary gifts
   - Mother's Day / Father's Day
   - Christmas / Holiday boxes
   - "Just Because" care packages
   - Corporate gift boxes
5. **Branding:** Wife creates logo, color palette, social media templates
6. **Launch Checklist:**
   - [ ] Products uploaded with descriptions and images
   - [ ] Payment processing enabled (Shopify Payments)
   - [ ] Shipping rates configured (free shipping threshold)
   - [ ] Legal pages (Privacy, Terms, Refund Policy)
   - [ ] Email flows set up (Welcome, Abandoned Cart, Post-Purchase)
   - [ ] Social media accounts created
   - [ ] Soft launch with friends & family

### Pricing Strategy

| Approach                | Details                                             |
|-------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| Cost-plus pricing       | 2.5x–3x supplier cost                              |
| Perceived value pricing | Bundle items to increase perceived value             |
| Free shipping threshold | $50+ orders get free shipping                       |
| Seasonal premiums       | 10-20% markup during holidays                        |
| Upsells                 | Gift wrapping ($5), cards ($3), rush shipping ($8)  |

### Giftifye Scaling Plan

- **Month 1-3:** Test 20-30 products, identify winners
- **Month 4-6:** Double down on top 5 products, add bundles
- **Month 7-9:** Add corporate gifting, scale ads for winners
- **Month 10-12:** Holiday push, maximum inventory/ad spend

## 5. Stream 4: Cookie Business (Shopify)

### Cookie Business Startup Steps

1. **Legal Requirements (CRITICAL):**
   - Research your municipality's cottage food / home bakery laws
   - Ontario: Check "Home-Based Food Business" rules under local public health unit
   - May need: Food Handler Certificate, home kitchen inspection, or business permit
   - Some municipalities allow "low-risk" baked goods (cookies qualify) under cottage food rules
   - **Action:** Call your local public health unit before selling

2. **Recipe Development:**
   - Perfect 3-5 core cookie recipes
   - Test shelf life and shipping durability
   - Get family/friends taste feedback
   - Document all recipes (business IP)

3. **Packaging & Branding:**
   - Wife designs cookie box branding
   - Source packaging: boxes, tissue paper, labels, stickers
   - Ensure packaging keeps cookies fresh during shipping
   - Include allergen information on labels (required)

4. **Shopify Setup:**
   - Option A: Separate Shopify store (CookieName.com)
   - Option B: Add as collection on Giftifye.com (cookie gift boxes)
   - Option C: Both — cross-promote between stores

5. **Pricing:**

| Product                    | Price  | Cost    | Margin |
|----------------------------|--------|---------|--------|
| 6-cookie box               | $18    | $5-7    | ~65%   |
| 12-cookie box              | $30    | $8-12   | ~63%   |
| Custom event cookies (dz)  | $40    | $12-15  | ~65%   |
| Monthly subscription       | $25/mo | $8-10   | ~64%   |
| Cookie + Gift combo        | $50    | $18-22  | ~58%   |

1. **Shipping:**
   - Local delivery: you deliver (deduct travel)
   - Canada Post: flat rate or free shipping over $50
   - Use proper food-safe shipping materials

### Cookie Business Scaling Plan

- **Month 1-2:** Recipe perfection, legal research, packaging design
- **Month 3-4:** Soft launch (friends, family, local Facebook groups)
- **Month 5-6:** Online launch, first paid ads
- **Month 7-9:** Scale based on demand, add seasonal varieties
- **Month 10-12:** Holiday cookie boxes, maximum production
