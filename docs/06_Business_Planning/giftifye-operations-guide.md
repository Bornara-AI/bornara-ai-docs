## 2. Product Research System

### Research Philosophy

Gift products are different from regular dropshipping. People buy gifts because of **emotion, not
utility**. Research must focus on:

- **Emotional triggers** — "This reminds me of them," "This is cute/thoughtful/unique"
- **High perceived value** — Bundles, packaging, personalization, aesthetic design
- **Occasions** — Birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, baby showers, teacher gifts
- **Visual appeal** — Wife's design skills are the competitive advantage

### 5-Source Product Research System

#### Source 1 — TikTok ("TikTok Made Me Buy It")

Search terms: "gift ideas 2026," "gift for girlfriend/boyfriend/mom/dad/baby," "gift bundle,"
"unboxing gift," "gift box aesthetic." Look for videos with 100k+ views and comments like
"I need this."

#### Source 2 — Etsy (Inspiration Only)

Search: "gift box," "gift set," "spa gift," "baby gift," "teacher gift," "personalized gift."
Look for best sellers, high review counts, aesthetic packaging, bundle ideas. Wife recreates
branding, packaging, and color themes.

#### Source 3 — Amazon Best Sellers

Categories: Gift sets, Beauty & personal care, Baby, Home decor, Pet supplies, Jewelry. Look for
"Amazon's Choice" with 4+ stars, items under $30 cost, items that can be bundled.

#### Source 4 — AliExpress / CJ Dropshipping

Look for fast shipping, good reviews, high-quality photos, variants, items that fit bundles.
Use CJ Dropshipping for faster shipping to Canada.

#### Source 5 — Seasonal Calendar

- February: Valentine's Day
- May: Mother's Day
- June: Father's Day
- September: Back to school
- November-December: Christmas

### Product Selection Criteria (7-Rule Filter)

Every product must pass ALL:

1. **Emotional appeal** — Does it make someone say "aww" or "wow"?
2. **High perceived value** — Looks like $50 even if it costs $10?
3. **Bundle-friendly** — Combinable with 2-4 other items?
4. **Aesthetic** — Looks good in photos and videos?
5. **Lightweight** — Cheaper shipping = higher margins
6. **Easy to personalize** — Optional but powerful
7. **Fits brand** — Soft, warm, thoughtful, aesthetic

## 3. Bundle Creation Guide

### Bundle Pricing Formula

```text
Bundle Cost x 3 = Selling Price
Example: Cost $12 → Sell $36 → Round to $39.99
Premium bundles: Cost x 4 or x 5 (especially personalized)
```

### Core Bundle Lineup

**1. Relax & Recharge Spa Box: $49.99** (Cost ~$12)

- Scented candle, bath bomb, face mask, mini lotion, greeting card

**2. Coffee Lover Starter Pack: $39.99** (Cost ~$10)

- Coffee mug, coffee sampler, biscotti, coaster, thank-you card

**3. Baby Welcome Gift Set: $59.99** (Cost ~$15)

- Baby socks, plush toy, milestone card, baby lotion, gift card

**4. Anniversary Date Night Box: $54.99** (Cost ~$14)

- Candle, chocolate, love note card, mini photo frame, rose soap

**5. Pet Parent Essentials Kit: $39.99** (Cost ~$10)

- Pet toy, treats, pet bandana, pet sticker

### Bundle Photography (Wife's Work)

- Use natural light
- Clean backgrounds with props (flowers, ribbon, confetti)
- Overhead shots + close-ups + lifestyle shots
- "What's inside the box" flat-lay layout
- Add text overlays for social media

### Bundle Description Formula

1. **Emotional hook:** "Make her day unforgettable with this curated spa box."
2. **What's inside:** List items clearly
3. **Why it's special:** "Hand-picked items she'll love."
4. **Occasions:** "Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays."
5. **Guarantee:** "Fast shipping + satisfaction guaranteed."

### Adding Bundles to Shopify

- Create new product with bundle images and "What's inside" graphic
- Add emotional description
- Add variants (with/without card)
- Add upsells: gift wrapping ($5), greeting card ($3), extra item
- Add to collections: Gifts for Her, Gifts for Him, Best Sellers, Seasonal

## 4. Supplier Vetting Checklist

### Supplier Reliability

- [ ] Rating: Minimum 4.6+ stars
- [ ] Orders: Minimum 500+ on the product
- [ ] Years: 2+ years on platform
- [ ] Response time: Within 24 hours
- [ ] Communication: Clear English, helpful

### Shipping & Delivery

- [ ] Offers ePacket, CJPacket, or local warehouse shipping
- [ ] Ships to Canada in 7-14 days (max 20)
- [ ] Full tracking provided
- [ ] Gift-friendly packaging available
- [ ] Reasonable shipping cost

### Product Quality

- [ ] Product reviews 4.6+ with photo reviews
- [ ] High-quality product photos, multiple angles
- [ ] Color/size variants available
- [ ] Sample ordered and tested by wife

### Red Flags (Skip These Suppliers)

- No reviews or fake reviews (all 5 stars, no photos)
- Slow response, no tracking, 20+ day shipping
- Poor packaging, inconsistent photos
- No refund policy, low order volume

## 7. Conversion Optimization Guide

### Conversion Philosophy

Gift buyers want: **Emotion, convenience, aesthetic presentation, fast answers, trust.** The store
must communicate: "This is the perfect gift — and it looks beautiful."

### Homepage Checklist

- [ ] Strong hero banner: "Curated Gifts for Every Moment"
- [ ] 3-5 featured bundles (best sellers)
- [ ] Seasonal section
- [ ] Testimonials (even 3-5 simple ones)
- [ ] Email signup: "Get 10% off your first gift box"
- [ ] Aesthetic photos
- [ ] Clear navigation by gift category

### Checkout Optimization (Reduce Abandonment)

- [ ] Enable Shop Pay, PayPal, Apple Pay
- [ ] Add trust badges and "Free shipping over $X"
- [ ] Gift message box at checkout
- [ ] No forced account creation, no unnecessary fields, no surprise fees

### Mobile Optimization (70-80% of traffic)

- [ ] All pages tested on mobile
- [ ] Large buttons, scrollable images
- [ ] Reduced text blocks
- [ ] Sticky add-to-cart button

### Email Conversion Flows

| Flow               | Emails | Purpose                                         |
|--------------------|--------|-------------------------------------------------|
| Abandoned Cart     | 3      | "Forgot something?" → "Still available" → 10% off|
| Welcome            | 1      | "Welcome to Giftifye — here's 10% off"          |
| Holiday            | Per    | Valentine's, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Christmas|
| Post-Purchase      | 2      | "Thank you!" → "How did they like the gift?"     |

### Conversion Targets

| Period          | Target Rate |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Month 1         | 1-1.5%      |
| Month 2-3       | 1.5-2.5%    |
| Month 4-6       | 2.5-3.5%    |
| Holiday season   | 3-5%        |

## 8. Advertising Strategy

### Platform Strategy

**TikTok Ads (Primary Channel):**

Content types: Unboxing videos, gift reactions, "What's inside?", seasonal trends, ASMR. Budget:
$5-$20/day testing, scale winners to $20-$50/day. Target: Women 25-45, gift shoppers.

**Facebook Ads:**

Carousel ads for bundles, retargeting visitors, lookalike audiences from buyer list.

**Instagram:**

Aesthetic product photos, Reels (repurpose TikTok), Stories (behind-the-scenes, polls).

### Revenue-Driven KPIs

| Metric              | Target                    |
|---------------------|---------------------------|
| ROAS                | 1.5-2.5                   |
| Cost per purchase   | $10-$25                   |
| AOV                 | $35-$60                   |
| CTR                 | > 1%                      |
| Add to cart rate    | > 5%                      |

### Scaling Rules

- Scale when: CTR > 1%, add-to-cart > 5%, purchases coming in, ROAS > 1.5
- How: Increase budget 20-30% every 2-3 days, duplicate winning ad sets, create lookalikes
- Kill when: No traction after $20-$40 spent
